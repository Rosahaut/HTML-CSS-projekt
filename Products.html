Monitors/Defibrillators

As a healthcare professional, you need a defibrillator/monitor with more advanced specifications than members of the public walking down the street. The well-known brands Zoll, Corpuls and Lifepak offer defibrillators/monitors with the advanced specifications that you need. Have a look at our selected devices.

X Series® is compact and lightweight and delivers the capabilities you expect from a full-featured defibrillator/monitor. When it comes to durability, the X Series is tested to a more challenging standard than any other monitor on the market. That’s because its durability is measured against the most current military-specified standards.

The next generation of corpuls3 offers an entirely new and revolutionary design-concept compared with traditional compact defibrillator/monitoring devices. The defibrillator/pacermodule SLIM reduces total weight to only 6.3 kg and enhances mobility and ergonomics.

Designed for patient transport and crash cart use, the LIFEPAK 20e is a defibrillator/monitor that is both powerful and easy to use. This defibrillator can be operated manually or automatically. Other operating modes include setup, service, and archival. The LIFEPAK 20e was made to be easy to use, for both BLS and ALS teams.
